>Lamborghini Reventón

"The chances of owning the new Reventón Roadster 1:700,000,000" - costing a cool $250,000 more than the Reventón this car is also limited to only 20 examples. With 40 years of building open-top cars, Roadsters are a way for Lamborghini to add that extra emotional component to their technological expertise. Where the engine in the Reventón was a tuned version of the LP 640's engine the Reventón Roadster features an engine almost identical to the one used in the hardcore LP 670-4 SuperVeloce. Unlike the Reventón the exclusive paint used has a matt grey finish that is brought to life in sunlight due to the inclusion of metal particles. The wheels are formed of 5 y-form spokes which include a carbon crescent-shaped fin that helps cool the brakes even further.

PRICE: R3 389 999
