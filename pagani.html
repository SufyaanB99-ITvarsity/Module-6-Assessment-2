Pagani Zonda Cinque Roadster

The Pagani dealer for Hong Kong had a very unique proposal, to be able to sell road legal Zonda R's. At a cost nearing $2000000 each only 5 cars were built which is why the car is named Cinque. With an obvious focus on performance the ride of the Zonda Cinque is very low. It is only through the use of exotic materials such as titanium and an aluminium/magnesium alloy that allows the chassis, suspension and wheels to perform like a race car on the uneven surfaces of everyday roads. The race influence can easily be seen in the external detailing of the Pagani Cinque - a longer front spoiler, new designed rear wing, overhead air intake, extra air intakes for the rear brakes.

PRICE: R3 214 999
